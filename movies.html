My Movie Recommendations

A collection of movies I've watched and my thoughts on them.

All-Time Favorites

The Matrix (1999)

A revolutionary film that perfectly blends high-concept science fiction with philosophy and groundbreaking action. It's the ultimate movie about questioning reality.

Inception (2010)

Christopher Nolan's masterpiece on the architecture of dreams. It's a complex, visually stunning thriller that stays with you long after the credits roll.

Interstellar (2014)

An emotional journey through space and time. The scientific accuracy combined with a powerful father-daughter story makes it one of my favorites.

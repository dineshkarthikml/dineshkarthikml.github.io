Courses

  • Applied Data Science for Industry: From Fundamentals to Deep Learning: This comprehensive training course centers around the practical application of data science in the industrial sector and the duration of the course will be 12-weeks. It provides a thorough exploration of fundamental concepts and their practical use cases in industries, with each topic being accompanied by the detailed hands-on implementation. The course will be facilitated by a team of industry and academic experts and researchers, who will also be available to address any additional queries or concerns. By enrolling in this course, you will be equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to apply for a variety of data- focused roles, including Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Data Analyst, and ML Engineer, across various industries. Additionally, we offer specialized courses and research projects that can elevate your career to the next level.
    Please reach out to us for the syllabus.

  • Advanced Deep Learning: Techniques and Applications: This course is designed for students, aspiring data scientist and ML engineers who want to gain a deeper understanding of the cutting-edge techniques and applications of deep learning. As one of the most rapidly evolving fields in computer science and artificial intelligence, deep learning has become an essential tool for solving a wide range of complex problems, from natural language processing and computer vision to robotics and self-driving cars. This course provides a comprehensive overview of the latest developments in deep learning, including advanced neural network architectures, generative models, graph neural networks, and more. Through a combination of lectures, hands-on exercises, and real-world projects, students will learn how to apply these techniques to tackle real-world problems and develop advanced deep learning applications. Whether you're a seasoned machine learning practitioner or just starting out, this course offers a unique opportunity to master the most advanced deep learning techniques and stay ahead of the curve in this rapidly evolving field.
    Please reach out to us for the syllabus.
